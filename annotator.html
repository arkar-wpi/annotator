Draw the direction of the gaze from the anywhere inside the bounding box surrounding the head

Click on the region inside the head bounding box. The bounding box will be selected. Drag the mouse pointer in the direction of the gaze and this will draw the gaze line. Drag until the region/object/person/face where the subject is looking. If the subject is looking out of the frame, click on the bounding box and then click inside the bounding box again. Select the respective attributes in the window which appears after you have lifted finished drawing the gaze line or clicked inside the bounding box again to indicate that the subject is looking at something not in the picture.
